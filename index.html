How It Works

Airbag Module Reset Service

Modern modules lock up after a deployment or even a minor bump. Dealerships replace them for $300-$800. I clear the crash data and hard-faults for $40 flat—the way it should be.

The Process

  1. Email or text your make, model, year, and part number.
  2. Ship the module or drop it off at the shop.
  3. I bench-reset it. Most units are done same-day.
  4. Pay $40. If it’s unrepairable, you only cover return shipping—no surprises.
  5. Re-install, clear any remaining seat-belt codes, and you’re back on the road.
